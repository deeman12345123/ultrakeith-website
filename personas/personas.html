DR. OCTAGON

1996 - Present

The most famous of Keith's personas, Dr. Octagon is a time-traveling gynecologist from Jupiter who performs bizarre medical procedures. Revolutionary abstract lyrics mixing medical terminology with sci-fi themes created an entirely new subgenre.

KEY RELEASES:

• Dr. Octagonecologyst (1996)
• The Return of Dr. Octagon (2006)
• Moosebumps: An Exploration Into Modern Day Horripilation (2018)

KOOL KEITH

1984 - Present

The core identity and original persona. Keith Thornton from the Bronx who started with Ultramagnetic MCs and revolutionized hip-hop with abstract lyrics, unconventional flows, and futuristic concepts. The foundation from which all other personas emerge.

KEY RELEASES:

• Sex Style (1997)
• Matthew (2000)
• Feature Magnetic (2016)
• 50+ albums as Kool Keith
